## About

I am a Research Engineer working at the intersection of image processing, applied mathematics, and computational signal analysis.

My work focuses on developing mathematically grounded image processing methods to extract reliable and quantifiable information from complex imaging data. I design robust computational models that transform raw signals into structured, interpretable representations suitable for analysis, measurement, and decision-making.
